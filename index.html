About Me

I’m Ravan — a frontend-focused creative developer with a passion for turning ideas into elegant, functional web experiences. I enjoy working across the full stack, but my strength lies in designing interactive, user-friendly interfaces that combine logic with emotion.

I’m motivated by creativity, real-world problem solving, and continuous learning. Every project is an opportunity to explore something new and sharpen my thinking. I care deeply about details and how they shape the overall experience.

My approach is shaped by four core values: simplicity, consistency, curiosity, and perfectionism. Whether I’m coding a complex feature or a small animation, I focus on clarity and user experience — both in the interface and behind the scenes.

My long-term goal is to become a senior developer and build products that are not only technically strong but also meaningful to people. I want to grow with teams that value creativity, clean code, and purposeful work.
